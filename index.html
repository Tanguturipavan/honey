Akhansha (Honey)

About

Aakanksha Honey is an Indian Instagram star who is known for the lip-sync, fashion, and family-oriented content that she uploads to her self-titled Instagram account.Aakanksha Honey is a Film Actress, who mainly works in the Telugu language. She was born and bought in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
                  In 2016, Aakanksha started her acting career with a web series ‘Parichayam Prema Parinayam’. Aakanksha then acted in various web series, short films & cover songs like Bava, O Pillo O Janamma, Janaki Samethasya, Naa Lover Bangaram, etc.
